Having grown up involved in sports and focused on a career in Mechanical Engineering, it was a surprise to many when Moriah decided to shift into an acting career. As a naturally soft-spoken and reserved introvert, acting isn't something most people think of when it comes to changing gears. But she has many layers, and beneath the surface there's booming personality, talent and drive to make a name for herself in the entertainment industry!

So, why acting? “From giving a gift, to sarcasm and silliness, making somebody smile, laugh, or positively impacting someone in some way is always a highlight of my day. Acting is a way to do this and reach more than the few I encounter on a regular basis. There's not much that I love more than bringing a character to life and being able to witness how I've impacted the audience. It's one of my greatest blessings and I pray my work is a blessing to everyone it touches in return”.
